Ingredients

  • Pizza base
  • Pizza sauce
  • Pepperoni
  • Mozarella

Steps

  1. Place your pizza base on a pizza pan
  2. Add a layer of pizza sauce
  3. Add a pepperoni to the pizza base
  4. Add your cheese to the pizza
  5. Bake for 15 minutes
