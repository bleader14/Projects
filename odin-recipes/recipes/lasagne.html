Ingredients

  • Lasagne Sheets
  • Bechamel
  • Beef Ragu
  • Cheese

Steps

  1. Place lasagne sheets in the dish
  2. Add a layer of ragu
  3. Add a layer of bechamel
  4. Reapeat steps 1-3 twice
  5. Add a final layer of cheese
  6. Bake for 40 minutes
